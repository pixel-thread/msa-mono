Complete Redesign Directions

Since we just moved to the Bento Box layout, let's explore completely different paradigms.

A

The Executive Feed

A chronological, vertical timeline of everything happening. Imagine a social media feed but for your tasks, meetings, and notifications. Very list-heavy, highly scannable, and prioritizes "what's next".

B

The Action Center

Extremely minimal. Large typography. Shows only the *single* most important thing you need to do right now (e.g., "Join Meeting" or "Complete Training"), with secondary items tucked behind a swipe or modal.

C

The Data Heavy View

Focuses on charts, graphs, and completion metrics. "80% of staff active", "3 trainings left". Relies on visual data representation rather than text lists. Good if the user acts as an administrator or manager.
